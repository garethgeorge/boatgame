THE FROZEN WASTE

Brave the freezing temperatures and avoid the treacherous icebergs.

  • Slippery Slaloms: Maneuver around giant icebergs to keep your boat intact.
  • Arctic Inhabitants: Look out for polar bears and penguins in their natural habitat.
  • Frozen Bottles: Some bottles might be harder to see against the snow and ice.

Stay warm and keep your eyes on the horizon!
