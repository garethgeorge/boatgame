THE MUDDY SWAMP

Keep your eyes peeled for ambushing predators in these murky waters.

  • Ambush Hazards: Watch out for alligators and snakes hiding in the reeds.
  • Dense Mangroves: The river banks are thick with mangrove roots; stay center!
  • Murky Treasure: Don't miss the bottles floating in the dark swamp water.

The swamp is full of secrets. Tread carefully!
