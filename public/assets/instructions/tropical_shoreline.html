TROPICAL SHORELINE

Welcome to the Tropical Shoreline! A sunny paradise awaits with new friends and sights.

  • Dolphins: Watch these playful creatures jump and swim in the open waters.
  • Turtles: Look for turtles resting near the sandy banks or swimming slowly.
  • Palm Trees: Enjoy the shade of the swaying palms along the shore.

Keep your eyes peeled for marine life as you navigate the coast!
