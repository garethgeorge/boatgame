THE SCORCHING DESERT

You've entered the desert biome. Watch out for the heat and unique hazards!

  • Collect Bottles: Gather floating bottles to increase your score and protection.
  • Find the Depot: Deliver your collected bottles to the bottle depot to bank your points.
  • Beware of Wildlife: Watch out for desert animals like scorpions and vultures!

The desert is vast, but stay on the river to survive!
